About Jungle Groves

Welcome to Jungle Groves. This project catalogs and tracks data related to unique fruit tree genetics, including my hybrid fig crosses like the $317 "Charlie" fig ($Ficus opposita \times Ficus carica$), and research into specialty crops that thrive in environments like the orange grove we purchased in Sebring, Florida. The long-term goal is to better understand traits like root-knot nematode (RKN) resistance.
